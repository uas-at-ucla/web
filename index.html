About Us
We are UAS@UCLA, the premier undergraduate aerial robotics team at UCLA. Our student-run team designs, manufactures, and tests cutting-edge drone hardware and software.
We bring new ideas to the world of UAVs by seeking out challenges that push us to expand what's possible- from search-and-rescue to long-endurance flight. We are ambitious undergraduates striving to grow this professional organization, dedicated to providing students a pathway to get involved with UAVs and foster valuable career skills.
